Relay Relay packs a full band punch into a tiny duo package. Hannah and Jamie have been performing professionally together for over 10 years, intertwining their own distinct sound throughout a super fun set of all bops and bangers — from Motown, nostalgic 90s, and 2000s Indie Rock to the Pop hits of today. They met while pursuing Bachelor of Music degrees at UNC Greensboro.

Greensboro, NC based — Available nationwide
