Our Story

Two voices,
one sound.

We're a husband and wife duo from North Carolina who fell in love over late-night jam sessions and never stopped playing. Relay Relay brings big-venue energy to every performance — from backyard weddings to packed club stages.

With a deep catalog spanning Motown soul, 90s indie anthems, and modern pop hits, we read the room and play what feels right. No rigid set list. Just music, connection, and a whole lot of heart.
