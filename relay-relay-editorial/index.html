About the duo

Relay Relay packs a full band punch into a tiny duo package. Hannah and Jamie have been wowing audiences for over 10 years with big sound and a unique set spanning Motown, nostalgic 90s, 2000s Indie Rock, and today's Pop hits.

Hannah

They met while pursuing their Bachelor of Music degrees at UNC Greensboro and have been performing professionally together ever since — bringing warmth, energy, and genuine musicianship to every stage they share.
